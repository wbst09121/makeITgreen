ABOUT THE GAME

Make it Green: Application game that teaches waste management in the society

The game “Make It Green” can be can be accessed by the user. The users can play the game by choosing worlds which contains different levels. After a level you can proceed to the next and after you finish the world you can choose another world to play. The learning outcomes from the game can be applied to into real life.

“Make It Green” can be a big help to eradicate the large amount of waste and pollution in our nature. “Make It Green” will promote knowledge about proper waste disposal and its harmful effects to us and make learning easier and fun. It can teach the younger generation more since they are much immersed into Video games.
