How To Play The Game

1

Make it Green is strategic game to protect our mother Earth using a trees animals and other good things against garbage.



2

A classic clash from the two sides: the “Nature Guardians” and “garbage”. The concept of this game is to defend our planet in from the garbage and harmful hazardous waste.

3

Each of the levels you passed will also have an upgrade. Moreover, the game will get harderand harder at each level. To claim the victory, defeat the invaders and bring back the green to our planet.
