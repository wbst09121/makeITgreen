Make It Green

Fight alongside the forces of Nature together with your allies of Trees, animals, and the 4 elements, Fire, Water, Air, and Earth, etc. And defeat the destroyer and invaders furthermore the forces of Darkness, e.g. plastics, cans, bottles, and other harmful and hazardous waste. And together let’s bring back the Light and green, “Make it Green”.

First Video

We all know that our planet is not that healthy as it uses to before, therefore a simple act such as creating a game like this “ make it green” will at least helps lessen our problem when it comes from the garbage in society or even worldwide. People nowadays especially kids are involving a lot of activities such as online games Internet browsing etc.

Second Video

This game will not only be enjoyed by the gamer, but it might teach them the right way of the proper way of disposing of their waste and make them aware of what is now happening on our planet. Hence, the use of this technique is to teach the process of proper throwing your garbage; disposal of non-biodegradable, biodegradable, and recycling some materials which can be used again.
