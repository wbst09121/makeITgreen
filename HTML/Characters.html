MEET THE CHARACTERS

TIGRES is one of the nature guardian from the Kingdom of Aetas. He is one of the bravest soldiers in their kingdom. Tigres has a power that can burn anything. He uses his power to burn the enemies who are trying to destroy the earth.

ACACIA is a nature guardian from the Empire of Camunia's northern valley, protecting the earth's lands. Acacia is one of the strongest protector of their kingdom. He is blessed with the power to use the land and the trees to protect the earth from the trash that causes climate change.

MAIA is one of the nature guardians of the Kingdom of Tver. He is the third child of the king and has been given a power. His power comes from the earth's air, so he can make tornadoes and use his wings to fight enemies.

LILAC is one of the Nature Guardians from Tairia Empire. Ever since she was a child, Lilac has been praying that she would grow strong and take up the important duty of protecting their tribe. From a young age, she demonstrated an exceptional talent for combat and strength far beyond her age. Within time, she became the tribe's strongest warrior. Because of her strength, she became one of the natural guardians.
